    • The project details

    Gallus project is the game I work on during my free time in collaboration with Gabriel Fontaine, 3D designer. We started to imagine this game in 2020 and we intend to finish this project with the ambition to raise funds on KickStarter.

    In this fantastic medieval world, the game starts on a small island full of secrets and mysteries. Following the main story, he will gradually be led to take part in the decisions and events that govern this small patch of land where live different factions. To progress and try to unravel the mysteries that this island reserves for him, he will have to take part in conflicts, make alliances, integrate a faction and live there according to the customs and habits. Although free to choose, he should not rest on his laurels if he wants to discover the secret of dungeons and evil entities that torment the inhabitants, because the world will not wait for him.

    The general concept revolves around the procedural generation of dungeons. On the island are arranged different types of them that the player will have to discover and face in order to progress and upgrade his equipment and skills. However, it will not be enough to complete them all only once... Indeed, the player will discover soon enough that an evil force is at work on this island and that these bastions filled with hostile creatures change every time someone manages to find the exit.
