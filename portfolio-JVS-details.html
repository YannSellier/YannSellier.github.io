Project: RAUM, Multiplayer space battle RTS game

  • Category: Video games
  • Client: personnal project
  • Period: june 2018 - april 2019

Most used tools

  • Unreal Engine 4
  • C++
  • Maya

    • The project details

    This multiplayer space battle game has as its central element a rather particular turn-by-turn system. Indeed, unlike classic games, players play at the same time and not one at a time. Although it seems strange at first glance, this system was designed to develop the strategic stakes of game.
    To achieve that the game is split in two phase:

  • Planification, where the players plan all their actions.
  • Execution, where all the previously planned actions roll at the same time for all the players.

    • Thus, this forces players to anticipate the moves of their opponents and can make emerge any new types of strategy that would not even be possible in many classic turn by turn game. Who knows what can happen on the other side while carefully planning a plan of attack or a simlpe move!
