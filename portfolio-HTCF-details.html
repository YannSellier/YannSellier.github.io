Project: How to cook french

  • Category: Web design
  • Client: Concours Trophées Ambassadeurs de la Région Ile-de-France
  • Period: january - june 2021
  • URL of the project: www.howtocookfrench.com



Most used tools

  • WordPress
  • PhpMyadmin for the database management
  • PlanetHoster as a webhost





    • The project details

    The concept of How to cook French is simple: allowing international students coming to Ile de France to discover the richness of French gastronomy without going broke.

    To achieve that we built our project around to main components.

    The first showcases the gastronomic richness of France by making French and typical Ile de France recipes available in English. The recipes are made with popular ingredients from the Ile de France. In addition, there are tips to adapt to some of the diets.

    The second axis promotes the best places to eat in Paris. We realized that it is extremely complex for foreigners to get the best addresses in Paris because this information usually circulates in a closed circuit. The idea is really to highlight the gastronomic assets of the Ile de France such as addresses where to buy seasonal and regional products or typical restaurants; all this while trying to respect the small budget of a student who is already burdened with expenses related to Parisian life. We are convinced that it is possible, even for a student, to eat well and especially "French". You just need to find the opportunities. We know that it is not so simple for a student, who finds himself immersed in a new and especially unknown environment, to know where to look.

    It is to bring a solution to these young people from all over the world who came to discover our region that we imagined "How to cook French", a platform that would gather our desires to share the experiences that we lived here in Ile de France.
