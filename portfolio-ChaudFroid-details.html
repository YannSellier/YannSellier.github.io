Project: ChaudFroid

  • Category: Game Jam on Unreal
  • Period: april 2018


Project details

With one of my good friend who is designer, we used to challenge ourselves by organising little private Game Jams. The concept is pretty simple, we have 24 hours to imagine and make a game from scratch with a given theme and we usually don't sleep until it is finished.

The theme was "Hot & Cold" hence the name of the game which litterally means that in french. We decided to make a local coop multiplayer platformer. The two players have to progress through the levels using their respective powers to help each other out. Each one of them is on seperate course and has his own powers. The top character controls the ice and can make bridges and platforms and interact with some objects on the level bellow to help the other player to progress. On the other side, the bottom character controls fire and can burn ennemies above him to protect the upper character. The game is a clever mix between skills and resolving puzzle in coop. In order to finish the five levels we managed to build during the game jam, you and your teammate have to work together or you will die in the flames or under a giant frozen spike ball.

We got the main core features of the game working and some really good looking 3D models under 24 hours so it is a real success for us. The fact that we even managed to build more than one test level is really satisfying. The purpose of these events was not to deliver a fully functionning game but was more to work on the idea of the game from a random theme to test our creativity and most of all have fun. Each time we did not manage to finish a game was not a failure at all because we learned a lot from our mistakes. Thanks to those game jams, each time I encounter a problem, it is either I have already been in that situation or I know how to get the solution I need on the internet. Practice is all and those first few years of game dev were a hell of a good practice for me I can tell you that.
